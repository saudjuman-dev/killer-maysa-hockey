6 Rules

  • Technique FIRST. Doing it wrong just teaches your body to be wrong.
  • Touch the puck every day. Stickhandling and shooting — home routine, no skipping.
  • After every coached session, do 5-20 min in the driveway with what they showed you.
  • No heavy weights. Bodyweight and quick movement.
  • No long runs. Short hard bursts, like a hockey shift.
  • Work hard for a block, then take a real break. Japan = zero hockey. That's on purpose.

Take Care of You

  • Sleep 8-9 hours. Bed by 9:30 on school nights.
  • Eat before training (snack + water). Eat within 30 min after.
  • If something hurts (sharp pain, not tired muscles), stop and tell Mom/Dad or coach.
  • Miss a day? No big deal. Don't double up. Just do the next one.
